Cookie Policy

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that websites save on the user's device. They can be technical (necessary for the site to work) or profiling cookies (used to track browsing habits for advertising or statistical purposes).

Which cookies this site uses

This site is an institutional showcase and does not use profiling cookies or any analytics or tracking tools.

The only data stored locally is a technical preference, saved in your browser's localStorage, which remembers whether you have already dismissed the cookie notice, so it is not shown on every visit. This data does not identify the user and is not shared with third parties.

Third-party services

  • Google Fonts — the typefaces are loaded from Google's servers; this request does not set profiling cookies but may involve the transmission of your IP address to Google.
  • FormSubmit — used only when you submit the contact form, to deliver the message.
  • DD Global Store image CDN — for loading product images.

How to manage cookies

As the site does not use profiling cookies, no granular consent is required. You can however block or delete cookies and local data at any time from your browser settings.
