Why DEDEX

The right component, inside and outside the drysuit.

A drysuit is a closed system: its seal depends on every single component. A seal that gives way at the wrist, a boot that comes unglued, a weld that opens up. In cold water these are not mere inconveniences, they are the point at which the dive ends.

DEDEX brings together in a single line the components needed to build and repair drysuits to a professional standard: latex and neoprene seals, boots and socks, tape and edging for every assembly technique, accessories. Each reference is chosen to do one precise job well, and to integrate with the materials and methods you already use in the workshop.

  • ✓

    Sealing as a priority

    Seals, wrist seals and neck seals designed to seal watertight and stay watertight over time, dive after dive.

  • ✓

    Construction compatibility

    Components that work with trilaminate, neoprene and technical fabrics, and with the most common gluing, welding and stitching techniques.

  • ✓

    Complete range

    From standard versions to high-density HD, with a wide choice of sizes, thicknesses and finishes for both bespoke and series-produced drysuits.

The brand

DEDEX, a line born in the workshop. Drysuits components

DEDEX is the line dedicated to drysuit components: seals, boots, socks, tape, edging and accessories gathered in a single technical catalogue, designed for those who build and repair drysuits professionally.

Behind the selection lies over a third of a century of experience in technical materials for water sports. It is from this work that the criterion arises by which every reference enters the catalogue: sealing, durability and compatibility with real assembly methods, verified first and foremost in the field.

DEDEX is not a consumer brand. It is a working tool for manufacturers, repair workshops, diving centres and technical divers who need the right component, available, with clear specifications.
